Your Support Changes Lives

Every donation, no matter the size, helps provide essential resources and support to matriculants who need it most. Small tools make a massive difference in the hands of a determined learner.

What We Need

Ways to Donate & Support

Your contributions directly impact learners' ability to complete matric with dignity and confidence.

Stationery

Pens, pencils, highlighters, erasers, rulers, and scientific calculators that are essential for daily learning and exam preparation.

Paper & Books

Exam pads, notebooks, textbooks, and organized file folders to help learners stay organized and prepared.

Study Materials

Previous year notes, past exam papers, summary guides, and subject-specific study resources.

School Essentials

School bags, lunchboxes, water bottles, and other essentials that help learners focus on their studies.

Impact

Where Your Donation Goes

70%

Direct Learner Support

Study materials, stationery, tutoring sessions, and resources that go directly into learners' hands.

20%

Program Development

Mentorship programs, emotional wellbeing support, and post-school readiness initiatives.

10%

Operations & Admin

Essential operational costs to ensure transparent, effective, and accountable management.
